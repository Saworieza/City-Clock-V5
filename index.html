ABOUT US

City clock started its operations in Kenya in 1984 and later spread its wings to East Africa region. We are currently based in Kenya, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, Burundi, Namibia and still growing.

OUR VISION

To expand in the african market in the next decade, with a focus on profitable growth through superior customer service, innovation, quality and commitment.

OUR GOAL

To exceed the expectations of every client by offering outstanding customer service, increased flexibility and greater value, thus optimizing opportunity and improving efficiency combined with our hands-on experience, ensures that our clients receive the most effective and proffesional service.

Use city clock Advertising

We are the only company in the region whose media is wholly reliant on Solar power allowing advertisers double benefit;as a functional medium for advertising as well as a CSR avenue-it does not cause environmental degradation. Interesting point to note, many believe that we are part of the local Authorities, but have no direct connection to them.

City Clock is a company of firsts' and the first Kenyan owned business to start the franchising of its business systems into the southern region of Africa. We believe that we shall create a foot print in the whole of Africa to become a Pan African branding partner. The Company has endured that it has great outreach in the region and in a survey carried out by the Advertising Practitioners Association, was voted the best outdoor medium in Kenya-a huge milestone.

We have also partnered with various local authorities and we are now responsible for maintaining iconic Tower Clocks in the region.

We have effective teams who have wholly embraced the company vision and are geared to its growth and expansion plans. One of the reasons that City Clock has been able to work so well with many organizations, including local Authorities, is that it maintains its units at the highest standards. Downtime following accidents or malfunctions is normally minimal. City clock has put systems in place to ensure immediate action as soon as a breakdown is reported. Repair and restoration is closely monitored and feedback is given to the client up to completion of the work.

Through relevant licensing authorities, we have prominent sites in different towns as well as that of the Airport Authorities; to install and brand units in various airport blue chip, but we also work with many SMEs to provide reliable platform for the showcase of their various products & services.
